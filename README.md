# greenthumb
We, Plantora, are a community of plant lovers who want to help grow and care for your plants. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, we have something for everyone.

Our app is a tool that provides complete plant identification and plant care. You can identify a plant by simply taking a picture of it and get a database of over 500,000 plants. You can also identify each of the diseases associated with your plant and find the right care and solution for it.

We also provide a range of calculators that help you find the optimal amount of water, soil, sunlight and pot needed for your plants. You can set daily reminders to monitor your plant's needs and never miss a watering or irrigation.

If you are looking for new plants to add to your collection, we can recommend the best plants for you based on your lifestyle and preferences. You can also connect with other plant enthusiasts and share your tips and experiences at our community events.

We believe that plants make our lives better and happier. That's why we created Plantora, to help you create a greener and healthier place for you and your loved ones.

Download the Plantora app today and join our growing community of plant lovers!
